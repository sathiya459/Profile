Investment Philosophy

Portfolio Strategy

01

Long-Term Compounding

All positions held with a minimum 3–5 year horizon. Short-term noise is ignored in favour of long-term business quality. LTCG tax benefits (₹1L annual exemption) are factored into the holding strategy.

02

Sector Gap Elimination

Every new addition fills an identified sector void. No sector exceeds 20% of portfolio weight. Banking, IT, Pharma, Infra, Defence, FMCG, Insurance, Real Estate and Renewables all represented.

03

Quality Over Quantity

Every stock must pass a three-parameter screen: Strong/Moderate past performance (>8% CAGR), Outperform/Neutral current performance, and Hold/Strong Buy future outlook. No Caution-rated stocks retained.

04

Thematic Coherence

Stocks are chosen to complement each other — not just diversify randomly. Defence (BEL + HAL + GRSE), Power chain (POWERGRID + CGPOWER + KEC + NTPC + WAAREEENER), Pharma (SUNPHARMA + DRREDDY + DIVISLAB) all form coherent themes.

05

Risk Management

15% stop-loss on all small cap positions. No single stock exceeds 8% of portfolio. Quarterly review of all holdings. Annual rebalancing to maintain 50/35/15 target allocation. Dividend reinvestment into the underweight tier.

06

India Growth Story

The portfolio is a concentrated bet on India's next decade — urbanisation, financialisation of savings, defence indigenisation, energy transition, digital infrastructure, and the rise of the Indian consumer. Every holding maps to at least one of these macro themes.
